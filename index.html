About Us

Kuma Cafe was inspired by a light novel series, "Kuma Kuma Kuma Bear" written by Kumanano and was published in 2015. Just like the cute lighthearted run of the mill isekai sci-fi anime, Kuma Cafe offers out of this world desserts which promises to satisfy not just your otherworldly sweet cravings but also your isekai fantasy.
